How it Works?

For Patients

Step 1: Account Creation and Login

Initiate the process by creating an account and subsequently proceeding to log in using your credentials.

Step 2: Search and Appointment Booking

Search for a doctor from the list and book an appointment.

Step 3: View Requested Appointments

You can view your requested appointments in the "Your Appointments" section.

Step 4: Appointment Confirmation

Once a doctor accepts your appointment, the chat section becomes unlocked related to it.

Step 5: Interaction in the Chat Section

In the chat section, engage with the doctor to acquire essential details such as precise timing, designated payment link, preferred conversation language, and any other relevant details to ensure a smooth and efficient consultation. Kindly ensure that payments are exclusively made through the authorized link provided by the Personal-Doctor organization.

Step 6: Payment Confirmation

After making a payment through Razorpay, please share a screenshot of the transaction in the chat section. Ensure that you provide the correct username of the doctor during your payment submission.

Step 7: Video Call Initiation

After approving your screenshot, the doctor will make a video call.

Step 8: Audio Call Option

If you only want to make an audio call, please don't allow your camera during the video call.

Step 9: Post-Consultation Ratings

After the video call, you have to give a rating for your experience with the doctor (the doctor will also give a rating to you).

Customer Support

If you encounter any issues with doctors, video calls, payments, or other concerns, contact us for assistance.

For Doctors

Step 1: Account Creation and approval

Visit the Doctor Application section, complete the credential form, and submit. Prepare your documents and certificates for an upcoming interview. We'll also collect important details, such as your schedule and bank information, during the process.

Step 2: Onboarding to Your Profile

Following the successful approval of your application, our team will promptly reach out to you to obtain the necessary details and facilitate the setup of your profile on our platform. Your username and password will be securely delivered to you via message or email. Feel free to log in and access your account at your earliest convenience.

Step 3: Appointment Acceptance

In the "Pending Requests" section, review, accept, or cancel appointment requests along with a brief description of the patient's problem.

Step 4: Interaction in the Chat Section

All accepted appointments are accessible in the "Upcoming Appointments" section of the "Doctor Dashboard." Engage with patients through the chat section, providing details such as exact timing and preferred conversation language.

Step 5: Payment Confirmation

Share the Razorpay payment link in the chat section. For a customized payment link based on your charges, feel free to contact us anytime.

Step 6: Video Call Initiation

After approving a screenshot provided by the patient in the chat section, initiate a video call.

Step 7: Audio Call Option

If you only want to make an audio call, please don't allow your camera during the video call.

Step 8: Post-Consultation Ratings

Following the video call, provide a rating for your experience with the patient. The patient will reciprocate with a rating for you.

Step 9: Receive Your Payment

Once you've provided a rating, our team checks your username on the Razorpay dashboard, and we promptly transfer the payment to you, deducting a 5% commission.

Customer Support

If you encounter any issues related to patients, video calls, payments, or other concerns, please contact us for assistance.
